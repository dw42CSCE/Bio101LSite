Project Introduction

This website explores the characteristics of Marburg's Virus, caused by the pathogen Marburg Marburgvirus. Marburg Virus (MBV) was discovered in 1967, following two simultaneous outbreaks in Germany and Serbia. MBV has a 88% fatality rate. (World Health Organization) You'll find information on its transmission, symptoms, immune response, treatments, and prevention strategies, supported with credible sources.
