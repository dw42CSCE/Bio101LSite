Treatment

How the Pathogen Causes Disease

There are no licensed treatments yet. However, there are some therapies that have been shown to be effective, with some listed below:

1. Small Interfering RNA: NP-718-LNP
Uses lipid nanoparticles to deliver siRNA to target the Marburg virus nucleoprotein gene. Studies show early treatment is effective with this method.

2. Nucleoside Analog Inhibitor: BCX4430 (Galidesivir)
Synthetic nucleoside analog designed to target and inhibit the viral RNA polymerase. Treatment between 1-48 hours has shown good results.

3. Synthetic Nucleoside Analog: Favipiravir (T-705)
Synthetic guanidine nucleoside analog with broad antiviral properties. Not quite as effective depending on how it was administered.
